# Health Insurance Analysis: Excel-SQL-PowerBI

**Tools**: Excel,SQL,Power BI

**Project description**: In this project, I created an interactive dashboard to analyze customer, health, and insurance data, helping to identify patterns in medical costs, claims, plan types, and regions.

**Problem statement**: Insurance companies often struggle to understand how customer health conditions, plan types, and regions affect medical costs, claims, and profitability Without proper analysis, it becomes difficult to identify high-risk customers, cost-driving factors, and areas of improvement

**Objective**: 
- To combine **customer, health, and policy data** into one dashboard.
- To understand **how health conditions affect medical costs and claims**.
- To compare **plan types and regions** based on performance.
- To identify **high-risk customers** for better decision-making.
               
**Project Highlights**: 
- Used **Excel & Power query** to clean and prepare the data.
- Solved business problems using **SQL queries**.
- Designed an **interactive dashboard in Power BI** to track key KPIs such as **Total Customers, Claims Paid,
  Average Premium and Medical Cost**.
- Performed **data analysis across multiple tables** (Customer, Health, and Policy data) to uncover meaningful insights.
- Performed **multi-dimensional analysis** across **regions, plan types, and network tier.**
                        
**Key Insights**: 
- **PPO** plan type has the highest demand across all regions.
- **EPO** plan type has the lowest average medical cost.
- **Central** region is the most profitable, with a total profit of 9,321,548.31.
- Only **5 customers** have low income but high medical costs.
- **Central** region pays the highest premiums compared to other regions.
- **Central** region has the highest number of claims.

**Recommendations**: 
- Focus more on **PPO plans** since most customers prefer them.
- Promote **EPO plans** as a cheaper option to attract more users.
- Pay more attention to the **Central region** as it gives high profit.
- Help **low-income customers with high medical costs** by offering better support or plans.
- Try to **reduce high claims in the Central region** by managing costs.
- Set **premium prices based on customer risk** to keep it fair.
